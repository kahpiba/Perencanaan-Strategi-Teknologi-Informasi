# PERTEMUAN 10: JUSTIFIKASI INVESTASI TI & TATA KELOLA TI

## I. FRAMEWORK INVESTASI (Data Terverifikasi)

### 1.1 Metode Analisis Finansial (Praktik Terbukti Industri)

**Metode-metode yang diakui industri:**

**1. ROI (Return on Investment)**
- Formula: ROI = (Manfaat Bersih / Total Investasi) × 100%
- Waktu perhitungan: Biasanya 3-5 tahun
- Sumber standar: APICS SCOR Model, Project Management Institute (PMI)

**2. NPV (Net Present Value)**
- Formula: NPV = Σ [CFt / (1 + r)^t] - Investasi Awal
- Discount rate: Khas 10-15% untuk investasi TI
- Sumber: Textbook Corporate Finance (Ross, Westerfield, Jaffe)

**3. IRR (Internal Rate of Return)**
- IRR: Discount rate dimana NPV = 0
- Aturan keputusan: IRR > Cost of Capital → Terima
- Sumber: Financial Management Standards

**Sumber Referensi:**
- Project Management Institute. (2021). A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK® Guide) - 6th Edition.
- APICS. (2023). Supply Chain Operations Reference (SCOR) Model.
- Ross, S. A., Westerfield, R. W., & Jaffe, J. F. (2023). Corporate Finance (13th Edition). McGraw-Hill.
