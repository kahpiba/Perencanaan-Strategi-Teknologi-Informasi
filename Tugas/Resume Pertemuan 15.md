
# PERTEMUAN 15: FINAL PROJECT - DOKUMEN STRATEGI TI KOMPREHENSIF

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Mengintegrasikan semua konsep dari Pertemuan 9-14 untuk mengembangkan Dokumen Strategi TI yang komprehensif dan actionable.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Output Final Project**

**Deliverables:**
1. Dokumen Strategi TI (25-40 halaman)
2. Presentasi Eksekutif (10-12 slide)
3. Supporting artifacts (portfolio matrix, roadmap, governance model, risk register)

**2. Struktur Dokumen Strategi TI (15 Bagian)**

| Bagian | Content | Halaman |
|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | Ringkasan Eksekutif | 2-3 |
| 2 | Latar Belakang & Tujuan | 2-3 |
| 3 | Profil Organisasi & Strategi Bisnis | 3-4 |
| 4 | Kondisi Saat Ini (As-Is) TI | 4-5 |
| 5 | Masalah, Kebutuhan & Peluang | 3-4 |
| 6 | Prinsip & Arah Strategis | 2-3 |
| 7 | Arsitektur Target (High-Level) | 3 |
| 8 | Portofolio Inisiatif & Prioritas | 3-4 |
| 9 | Roadmap Implementasi | 2-3 + Gantt |
| 10 | Tata Kelola, Kebijakan & Keamanan | 2-3 |
| 11 | Rencana Anggaran & Sumber Daya | 2 |
| 12 | Risiko & Kontinjensi | 2 |
| 13 | Manajemen Perubahan (OCM) | 2 |
| 14 | KPI & Manajemen Benefit | 1-2 |
| 15 | Lampiran & Supporting Docs | Reference |
| | **TOTAL** | **40+ pages** |

**3. Proses 17 Tahap Pengembangan (4 Minggu)**

**MINGGU 1: Analisis Bisnis & AS-IS**

| Tahap | Aktivitas | Deliverables |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | Kickoff & Ruang Lingkup | Project Charter, stakeholder list, RACI |
| 2 | Pemahaman Bisnis | Business strategy summary, strategic requirements |
| 3 | Assessment AS-IS TI | Application catalog, system map, cost summary |
| 4 | Kematangan & Risiko | Maturity baseline, initial risk register |

**MINGGU 2: Strategi & Arsitektur**

| Tahap | Aktivitas | Deliverables |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| 5 | Kebutuhan & Masalah | Requirements backlog, prioritized problems |
| 6 | Prinsip & Arah | Strategic principles, strategic themes |
| 7 | Arsitektur Target | Architecture blueprint, design principles |

**MINGGU 3: Portfolio & Prioritas**

| Tahap | Aktivitas | Deliverables |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| 8 | Portofolio Inisiatif | Initiative catalog, McFarlan Grid |
| 9 | Prioritisasi & Business Case | Priority matrix, business case per initiative |

**MINGGU 4: Roadmap & Finalisasi**

| Tahap | Aktivitas | Deliverables |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| 10 | Roadmap Implementasi | Timeline, milestones, Gantt chart |
| 11 | Tata Kelola & Operating Model | Governance structure, RACI, decision framework |
| 12 | Kebijakan, Keamanan & Compliance | Policy map, security framework, compliance gap |
| 13 | Manajemen Perubahan | OCM strategy, communication plan |
| 14 | KPI & Benefits | Balanced scorecard, benefit tracking plan |
| 15 | Anggaran & Sourcing | Budget breakdown, sourcing strategy |
| 16 | Risiko & Kontinjensi | Top-10 risk list, mitigation plans |
| 17 | Kompilasi & Kualitas | Final document, quality review |

**4. Rubrik Penilaian (100%)**

| Komponen | Bobot |
|----------|-------|
| Definisi masalah & konteks bisnis | 10% |
| Analisis As-Is & kebutuhan | 20% |
| Strategi, prinsip & arsitektur target | 20% |
| Portfolio, prioritas & business case | 20% |
| Roadmap, tata kelola, risiko & KPI | 20% |
| Kualitas dokumen & presentasi | 10% |

**5. Key Success Factors**

✅ **Clear Problem Definition** → Understand business context & challenges
✅ **Comprehensive Analysis** → Honest assessment of current state
✅ **Strategic Clarity** → Clear direction aligned with business strategy
✅ **Sound Business Cases** → Justified investments dengan realistic numbers
✅ **Actionable Roadmap** → Realistic timeline dengan clear milestones
✅ **Strong Governance** → Clear decision rights & accountability
✅ **Risk Management** → Top risks identified dengan mitigation
✅ **Benefit Tracking** → Measurable KPIs untuk success assessment
✅ **Executive Alignment** → Stakeholder buy-in & support
✅ **Quality Documentation** → Professional, clear, well-organized

### Key Insights Pertemuan 15

✅ Final project integrates semua concepts dari Pertemuan 9-14
✅ Systematic 17-stage process ensures comprehensive coverage
✅ Document harus balance teori dengan praktik
✅ Governance & risk management non-negotiable
✅ Clear linkage antara business strategy & TI strategy critical
✅ Roadmap harus phased dengan quick wins untuk momentum
✅ Benefits realization tracking as important as implementation
