# PERTEMUAN 10: JUSTIFIKASI INVESTASI TI & TATA KELOLA TI

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Bagaimana organisasi dapat membenarkan investasi TI secara finansial dan memastikan tata kelola yang efektif untuk realisasi value.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Financial Analysis Methods (Standar Industri)**

**ROI (Return on Investment)**
- Formula: ROI = (Net Benefits / Total Investment) × 100%
- Waktu: Typically 3-5 years
- Keputusan: ROI >15-20% generally acceptable

**NPV (Net Present Value)**
- Formula: NPV = Σ [CFt / (1 + r)^t] - Initial Investment
- Discount rate: Typical 10-15% untuk TI investments
- Keputusan: NPV >0 → Accept

**IRR (Internal Rate of Return)**
- IRR: Discount rate dimana NPV = 0
- Keputusan: IRR > Cost of Capital → Accept

**2. Non-Financial Benefits Analysis**
- Sulit mengukur secara objektif (customer satisfaction, competitive advantage, risk mitigation)
- Persyaratan: Proxy metrics dan qualitative assessment
- Challenges: Attribution problem, long realization timeline

**3. Business Case Development**
- Identify investment opportunity & business drivers
- Define objectives & constraints
- Specify business & technical requirements
- Calculate financial metrics (ROI, NPV, IRR)
- Assess non-financial benefits
- Evaluate risks & mitigation
- Develop implementation plan

**4. IT Governance Framework**

Framework terbaik: Kombinasi dari:
- **COBIT** (governance objectives & controls)
- **ITIL** (service management)
- **ISO/IEC 38500** (board-level governance)
- **ISO 27001** (security management)

Governance Pillars:
1. **Strategic Alignment** → TI-bisnis alignment, portfolio management, benefits tracking
2. **Value Delivery** → Resource optimization, innovation management, quality assurance
3. **Risk Management** → Operational risk, compliance risk, strategic risk
4. **Performance Management** → KPI definition, balanced scorecard, benchmarking

### Studi Kasus 1: PT Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI)

**Organisasi:** BRI - Bank terbesar Indonesia (Rp 1.376,9T aset, 106 juta nasabah)

**Investment Opportunity:** Core Banking System Modernization
- Legacy system 15+ tahun, performance issues, scalability limitations
- Regulatory compliance gaps, operational risk

**Investment Options Analysis:**
1. In-house development: Rp 300B, 48 bulan, very high risk
2. System integrator: Rp 180B, 36 bulan, medium risk
3. Cloud-based platform: **Rp 120B, 24 bulan, medium-low risk** → SELECTED

**Financial Justification:**

Investment Breakdown:
- Software licenses & implementation: Rp 50B
- Infrastructure & cloud services: Rp 40B
- Training & change management: Rp 15B
- Contingency (15%): Rp 15B
- **Total: Rp 120B**

Benefits Realization (3 years):

| Benefit | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | Total |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| Cost reduction | Rp 13B | Rp 18B | Rp 20B | Rp 51B |
| Revenue enhancement | Rp 20B | Rp 43B | Rp 60B | Rp 123B |
| Risk mitigation value | Rp 5B | Rp 5B | Rp 5B | Rp 15B |
| **TOTAL BENEFITS** | **Rp 38B** | **Rp 66B** | **Rp 85B** | **Rp 189B** |

**Financial Metrics:**
- Year 1 ROI: 31.7%
- Year 2 ROI: 55%
- Year 3 ROI: 70.8%
- NPV (10% discount): Rp 32.8B ✓ POSITIVE
- IRR: ~28% (>10% cost of capital) ✓
- Payback period: 2.2 years ✓

**Implementation Governance:**
- CIO Office, Investment Committee, PMO oversee
- Risk committee monthly review
- Benefits realization tracking quarterly
- Post-implementation audit 3-6 months

### Studi Kasus 2: PT Telkom Indonesia

**Organisasi:** Telkom - Incumbent telecom (Rp 164,1T revenue, 205M pelanggan)

**Investment:** Digital Ecosystem Platform (Rp 500B, 3-year program)

**Strategic Rationale:**
- Respond to OTT competition
- Create new revenue streams
- Unlock value from 200M+ subscriber base
- Position as digital infrastructure provider

**Financial Projection (5-year):**
- Broadband subscribers: 18M → 50M
- Digital services revenue: Rp 500B → Rp 2.5T
- Operating margin: 25% → 35%
- NPV (12% discount): **Rp 2.988T** ✓ HIGHLY POSITIVE

**IT Governance Structure:**
```
Board of Directors
    ↓
Digital Transformation Committee
    ↓
├─ IT Governance Council (COBIT-based)
├─ Risk & Compliance Committee
└─ PMO & Delivery
```

**COBIT 2019 Implementation:**
- EDM01: Governance framework
- EDM02: Strategy aligned dengan business
- EDM03: Risk oversight
- EDM04: Resource optimization
- APO02-APO04: Strategic planning, architecture, innovation
- BAI01: Program & project management
- DSS01: Service management
- MEA01: Performance monitoring

### Key Insights Pertemuan 10

✅ Investment justification requires both financial & non-financial analysis
✅ IT governance critical untuk value realization
✅ Strong governance increases benefit realization probability
✅ Business case development must be rigorous
✅ Post-implementation benefits tracking essential
✅ Risk management & mitigation bukan optional
