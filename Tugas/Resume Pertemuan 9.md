# PERTEMUAN 9: PERENCANAAN KAPABILITAS TI & ROADMAP IMPLEMENTASI

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Bagaimana organisasi dapat mengevaluasi dan meningkatkan kapabilitas TI secara strategis melalui perencanaan roadmap yang terstruktur.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Definisi Kapabilitas TI**
- Kemampuan organisasi menggunakan teknologi informasi untuk mendukung strategi bisnis dan menciptakan nilai tambah
- Bukan hanya infrastruktur teknis, tetapi kombinasi dari:
  - Infrastruktur TI (hardware, software, jaringan, data center)
  - Manajemen Data & Informasi (kualitas data, integrasi sistem)
  - Aplikasi & Layanan Digital (mobile apps, cloud, AI/ML)
  - Sumber Daya Manusia TI (kompetensi, pelatihan, budaya inovasi)
  - Tata Kelola & Manajemen TI (alignment, standar, compliance)

**2. Lima Dimensi Kapabilitas TI**

| Dimensi | Fokus | Indikator Kunci |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Infrastruktur TI** | Hardware, software, jaringan, data center | Uptime 99.9%, disaster recovery, bandwidth |
| **Manajemen Data** | Integrasi sistem, kualitas data, governance | Data quality score >95%, integration 100% |
| **Aplikasi Digital** | Cloud adoption, AI/ML, user experience | Cloud workload >70%, AI automation |
| **SDM TI** | Kompetensi, pelatihan, talent management | Certification rate, training hours, turnover |
| **Tata Kelola TI** | Alignment bisnis-TI, compliance, risk | Alignment score, audit compliance, incidents |

**3. Metodologi Penyusunan Roadmap TI**

Tahapan sistematis:
1. **Analisis Kebutuhan Bisnis** → Memahami visi, strategi, tujuan, tantangan organisasi
2. **Evaluasi Kapabilitas As-Is** → Assessment mendalam kondisi TI saat ini
3. **Definisi Kapabilitas To-Be** → Target kapabilitas ideal yang ingin dicapai
4. **Identifikasi Gap Kapabilitas** → Analisis kesenjangan per dimensi
5. **Penyusunan Roadmap Implementasi** → Fase, timeline, budget, KPI keberhasilan

### Studi Kasus 1: PT Pertamina - Digitalisasi Supply Chain

**Organisasi:** PT Pertamina (Persero) - BUMN terbesar Indonesia (39.900 karyawan)

**Tantangan As-Is:**
- Sistem supply chain fragmented (procurement, inventory, logistics terpisah)
- Data silos menyebabkan visibility rendah
- Lead time distribusi tinggi, inventory carrying costs besar
- Risiko supply disruption tidak teridentifikasi optimal
- Skills gap dalam cloud, IoT, analytics

**Kapabilitas As-Is Assessment:**
- Infrastruktur TI: Level 2 (60%)
- Manajemen Data: Level 2 (55%)
- Aplikasi Digital: Level 1-2 (45%)
- SDM TI: Level 2 (50%)
- Tata Kelola TI: Level 2 (55%)
- **Average: 53%**

**Target Kapabilitas (5 Tahun):**
- Infrastruktur TI: Level 3-4 (85%) - gap +25%
- Manajemen Data: Level 3 (80%) - gap +25%
- Aplikasi Digital: Level 3 (75%) - gap +30%
- SDM TI: Level 3 (80%) - gap +30%
- Tata Kelola TI: Level 3-4 (85%) - gap +30%
- **Target Average: 80%**

**Roadmap Implementasi (3 Fase, 60 Bulan):**

| Fase | Duration | Budget | Focus | Key Initiatives |
|------|----------|--------|-------|-----------------|
| **Phase 1: Foundation & Quick Wins** | M1-12 | USD 15-20M | Momentum building | ERP cloud, IoT pilot, data governance, talent program, quick win analytics |
| **Phase 2: Acceleration & Transformation** | M13-30 | USD 25-30M | Strategic acceleration | Advanced analytics, cloud migration, supply chain optimization, RPA, digital culture |
| **Phase 3: Optimization & Scale** | M31-60 | USD 20-25M | Maturity & continuous improvement | Blockchain traceability, edge computing, digital ecosystem, continuous innovation |

**KPI & Metrics Kesuksesan:**
- Supply chain lead time: 30 hari → 15 hari
- Inventory turnover: 4x/tahun → 8x/tahun
- On-time delivery: 85% → 95%
- Supply chain cost/revenue: 8% → 5%
- Cloud workload: 15% → 80%
- Employee digital skills: 25% → 85%

### Studi Kasus 2: Gojek - Kapabilitas Analitik Data

**Organisasi:** Gojek (Unicorn Indonesia) - Super-app dengan 68 juta pengguna, 2+ juta transaksi harian

**Kapabilitas TI Current State:** Level 4 (Managed & Optimized) ~75-80%

**Analytics Capability - Key Use Cases:**

1. **Real-time Driver-Customer Matching**
   - Problem: Minimize wait time, optimize driver earnings
   - Solution: ML models predict demand, real-time location tracking, algorithm matching
   - Expected outcome: Average wait time <5 menit, driver utilization >80%

2. **Demand Forecasting & Supply Planning**
   - Problem: Predict demand spikes untuk optimal supply positioning
   - Solution: Time-series forecasting, exogenous variables, geo-spatial analysis
   - Expected outcome: Driver earnings stability +15%, satisfaction +20%

3. **Dynamic Pricing Optimization**
   - Problem: Maximize revenue, maintain customer satisfaction
   - Solution: Pricing algorithm based on demand/supply, A/B testing, personalization
   - Expected outcome: Revenue +8-12%, margin +5-8%, LTV +10%

4. **Fraud Detection & Risk Scoring**
   - Problem: Minimize fraud transactions, protect ecosystem
   - Solution: Anomaly detection models, real-time risk scoring
   - Expected outcome: Fraud prevention >99%, false positive <1%

**Data Analytics Roadmap (2025-2027):**
- Phase 1: Advanced Personalization (2025) → Deep learning, cross-service engine, engagement +30%
- Phase 2: Real-time Decision Engine (2025-2026) → Edge computing, RL pricing, conversational AI, efficiency +25%
- Phase 3: Ecosystem Analytics (2026-2027) → Partner analytics, network effects, sustainability

### Key Insights Pertemuan 9

✅ Kapabilitas TI harus direncanakan secara sistematis dengan gap analysis yang jelas
✅ Roadmap harus phased (quick wins → acceleration → optimization)
✅ Investment dalam SDM sama pentingnya dengan teknologi
✅ KPI & metrics essential untuk tracking kesuksesan
✅ Context matters: Traditional industries ≠ tech companies dalam trajectory
