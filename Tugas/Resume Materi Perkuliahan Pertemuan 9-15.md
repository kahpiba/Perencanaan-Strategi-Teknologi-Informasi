# PERTEMUAN 9: PERENCANAAN KAPABILITAS TI & ROADMAP IMPLEMENTASI

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Bagaimana organisasi dapat mengevaluasi dan meningkatkan kapabilitas TI secara strategis melalui perencanaan roadmap yang terstruktur.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Definisi Kapabilitas TI**
- Kemampuan organisasi menggunakan teknologi informasi untuk mendukung strategi bisnis dan menciptakan nilai tambah
- Bukan hanya infrastruktur teknis, tetapi kombinasi dari:
  - Infrastruktur TI (hardware, software, jaringan, data center)
  - Manajemen Data & Informasi (kualitas data, integrasi sistem)
  - Aplikasi & Layanan Digital (mobile apps, cloud, AI/ML)
  - Sumber Daya Manusia TI (kompetensi, pelatihan, budaya inovasi)
  - Tata Kelola & Manajemen TI (alignment, standar, compliance)

**2. Lima Dimensi Kapabilitas TI**

| Dimensi | Fokus | Indikator Kunci |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Infrastruktur TI** | Hardware, software, jaringan, data center | Uptime 99.9%, disaster recovery, bandwidth |
| **Manajemen Data** | Integrasi sistem, kualitas data, governance | Data quality score >95%, integration 100% |
| **Aplikasi Digital** | Cloud adoption, AI/ML, user experience | Cloud workload >70%, AI automation |
| **SDM TI** | Kompetensi, pelatihan, talent management | Certification rate, training hours, turnover |
| **Tata Kelola TI** | Alignment bisnis-TI, compliance, risk | Alignment score, audit compliance, incidents |

**3. Metodologi Penyusunan Roadmap TI**

Tahapan sistematis:
1. **Analisis Kebutuhan Bisnis** → Memahami visi, strategi, tujuan, tantangan organisasi
2. **Evaluasi Kapabilitas As-Is** → Assessment mendalam kondisi TI saat ini
3. **Definisi Kapabilitas To-Be** → Target kapabilitas ideal yang ingin dicapai
4. **Identifikasi Gap Kapabilitas** → Analisis kesenjangan per dimensi
5. **Penyusunan Roadmap Implementasi** → Fase, timeline, budget, KPI keberhasilan

# PERTEMUAN 10: JUSTIFIKASI INVESTASI TI & TATA KELOLA TI

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Bagaimana organisasi dapat membenarkan investasi TI secara finansial dan memastikan tata kelola yang efektif untuk realisasi value.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Financial Analysis Methods (Standar Industri)**

**ROI (Return on Investment)**
- Formula: ROI = (Net Benefits / Total Investment) × 100%
- Waktu: Typically 3-5 years
- Keputusan: ROI >15-20% generally acceptable

**NPV (Net Present Value)**
- Formula: NPV = Σ [CFt / (1 + r)^t] - Initial Investment
- Discount rate: Typical 10-15% untuk TI investments
- Keputusan: NPV >0 → Accept

**IRR (Internal Rate of Return)**
- IRR: Discount rate dimana NPV = 0
- Keputusan: IRR > Cost of Capital → Accept

**2. Non-Financial Benefits Analysis**
- Sulit mengukur secara objektif (customer satisfaction, competitive advantage, risk mitigation)
- Persyaratan: Proxy metrics dan qualitative assessment
- Challenges: Attribution problem, long realization timeline

**3. Business Case Development**
- Identify investment opportunity & business drivers
- Define objectives & constraints
- Specify business & technical requirements
- Calculate financial metrics (ROI, NPV, IRR)
- Assess non-financial benefits
- Evaluate risks & mitigation
- Develop implementation plan

**4. IT Governance Framework**

Framework terbaik: Kombinasi dari:
- **COBIT** (governance objectives & controls)
- **ITIL** (service management)
- **ISO/IEC 38500** (board-level governance)
- **ISO 27001** (security management)

Governance Pillars:
1. **Strategic Alignment** → TI-bisnis alignment, portfolio management, benefits tracking
2. **Value Delivery** → Resource optimization, innovation management, quality assurance
3. **Risk Management** → Operational risk, compliance risk, strategic risk
4. **Performance Management** → KPI definition, balanced scorecard, benchmarking

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Membandingkan dan menganalisis strategi TI di sektor publik vs sektor privat, dengan memahami drivers, challenges, dan best practices masing-masing.

| Dimensi | Sektor Publik | Sektor Privat |

# PERTEMUAN 12: TEKNIK PSSI & FRAMEWORK STRATEGI TI

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Teknik-teknik dan framework-framework yang digunakan dalam Perencanaan Strategis Sistem Informasi (PSSI) untuk alignment strategi bisnis-TI yang efektif.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Teknik Pendukung PSSI**

**A. SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)**

| Quadrant | Internal | External |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Positive** | Strengths (tangible assets, capabilities, competitive edges) | Opportunities (market trends, growth potentials, partnerships) |
| **Negative** | Weaknesses (limitations, gaps, deficiencies) | Threats (competitive threats, disruptions, regulatory changes) |

**Aplikasi dalam PSSI:**
- Identify strengths dalam teknologi existing untuk leverage
- Highlight weaknesses (skill gaps, legacy systems) untuk address
- Exploit opportunities (emerging tech, new markets)
- Mitigate threats (disruptive competitors, regulation)

**B. PESTEL Analysis (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal)**

| Factor | Analysis |
|--------|----------|
| **Political** | Government policies, trade regulations, political stability |
| **Economic** | Economic growth, interest rates, currency, inflation, spending |
| **Social** | Consumer preferences, education, demographics, culture, social media |
| **Technological** | Digital transformation pace, cloud adoption, AI, cybersecurity, legacy obsolescence |
| **Environmental** | Sustainability requirements, climate impacts, resource scarcity, green tech |
| **Legal** | Data protection (GDPR, PDPA), industry regulations, intellectual property |

**Aplikasi dalam PSSI:**
- Understand macro environment affecting TI strategy
- Identify regulatory requirements (compliance must-haves)
- Spot technology opportunities & threats
- Assess stakeholder expectations

**C. Porter's Five Forces (Competitive Analysis)**

| Force | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| **Threat of New Entrants** | Barriers to entry, capital requirements, channel access |
| **Bargaining Power of Suppliers** | Supplier concentration, switching costs, differentiation |
| **Threat of Substitutes** | Alternative solutions, price-to-value ratios |
| **Competitive Rivalry** | Market growth, product differentiation, competitive intensity |
| **Bargaining Power of Buyers** | Buyer concentration, switching costs, price sensitivity |

**Aplikasi dalam PSSI:**
- High threat new entrants → Need TI differentiation strategy
- Supplier power → Vendor management, multi-sourcing
- Substitutes high → Need continuous innovation
- Rivalry intense → TI as competitive advantage critical
- Buyer power high → Personalization, customer experience critical

**D. Critical Success Factors (CSF)**

**Proses Identifikasi:**
```
Strategic Objectives
    ↓
What must we do well?
    ↓
Critical Success Factors (3-5 key factors)
    ↓
Translate to KPIs
    ↓
Assign to initiatives & projects
```

**Contoh untuk Organisasi University:**
- CSF: Strong academic programs → KPI: Accreditation pass rate >95%
- CSF: Enhanced brand reputation → KPI: Ranking improvement, faculty publications
- CSF: Excellent student experience → KPI: Graduation rate >85%, alumni satisfaction
- CSF: Cost efficiency → KPI: Operating margin improvement
- CSF: Digital infrastructure → KPI: LMS adoption >90%, online course quality

**E. Balanced Scorecard (BSC)**

**4 Perspectives:**

| Perspective | Question | Examples |
|------------|----------|----------|
| **Financial** | How do we look to shareholders? | Revenue growth, profit margin, ROI, cost efficiency |
| **Customer** | How do customers see us? | Customer satisfaction, market share, brand value, retention |
| **Internal Process** | What must we excel at? | Operational efficiency, quality, cycle time, innovation |
| **Learning & Growth** | How can we improve? | Employee skills, technology infrastructure, culture, innovation |

**Linkage ke Strategy:**
- Strategic objective → Cascaded to 4 perspectives
- Each perspective has KPIs → Linked to initiatives
- Quarterly/annual reviews → Monitor progress & adjust

**2. Strategic Frameworks**

**A. Strategic Alignment Model (SAM) - Henderson & Venkatraman (1993)**

**4 Dimensions:**

```
BUSINESS DOMAIN
├─ Business Strategy (External)
└─ Business Infrastructure (Internal)

IT DOMAIN
├─ IT Strategy (External)
└─ IT Infrastructure (Internal)
```

**Alignment Objectives:**
1. **Strategic Fit:** Business strategy ↔ IT strategy
2. **Functional Integration:** Business infrastructure ↔ IT infrastructure
3. **Backward Linkage:** IT strategy influences business strategy
4. **Forward Linkage:** Business infrastructure enables IT infrastructure

**Contoh Alignment:**
```
Business Strategy: Focus premium segment, differentiation
                        ↓
IT Strategy: Digital channels, personalization capability
                        ↓
Business Infrastructure: Custom services, flexible operations
                        ↓
IT Infrastructure: Cloud systems, big data analytics
```

**B. Ward & Peppard Framework (2016)**

**4 Environment Analysis:**

```
Internal Business Environment
├─ Organization structure, culture
├─ Current strategies
├─ Value chain
└─ Strengths/weaknesses

External Business Environment
├─ Market conditions, competition
├─ Industry trends
├─ Regulatory, economic factors
└─ Opportunities/threats
            ↓
    STRATEGY FORMULATION
            ↓
Internal IT Environment
├─ Current TI assets, capabilities
├─ IT organization maturity
├─ IT skills, resources
└─ Systems, infrastructure

External IT Environment
├─ Technology trends
├─ Vendor landscape
├─ Industry best practices
└─ Talent market
```

**Output: 3 Strategies**
1. **SI Strategy** → What information systems needed?
2. **TI Strategy** → What technology infrastructure required?
3. **IS/IT Management Strategy** → How to manage SI/TI?

**C. COBIT 2019 Framework (ISACA)**

**Governance & Management Objectives:**

```
GOVERNANCE OBJECTIVES (EDM) - 5 objectives
├─ EDM01: Evaluated and Directed
├─ EDM02: Strategy Directed
├─ EDM03: Risk Oversight
├─ EDM04: Resource Optimized
└─ EDM05: Stakeholder Value Delivery

MANAGEMENT OBJECTIVES - 23 objectives across 4 domains
├─ ALIGN (APO): 13 objectives
│  ├─ APO01: Managed I&T Management Framework
│  ├─ APO02: Managed Strategy
│  ├─ APO03: Managed Enterprise Architecture
│  ├─ APO04: Managed Innovation
│  └─ ... (more)
├─ PLAN (BAI): 10 objectives
│  ├─ BAI01: Managed Programs & Projects
│  └─ ... (more)
├─ OPERATE (DSS): 6 objectives
│  ├─ DSS01: Managed Services
│  └─ ... (more)
└─ MONITOR (MEA): 4 objectives
   ├─ MEA01: Monitored & Evaluated IT Performance
   └─ ... (more)

ENABLERS - 7 categories
├─ Processes
├─ Organizational structures
├─ Culture, ethics, behavior
├─ Information
├─ Services, infrastructure, applications
├─ People, skills, competencies
└─ Technology, tools
```

**Maturity Model (0-5):**
- Level 0: Incomplete
- Level 1: Performed (Informal)
- Level 2: Managed (Basic discipline)
- Level 3: Defined (Standardized)
- Level 4: Managed & Measured (Controlled)
- Level 5: Optimized (Continuous improvement)

### Key Insights Pertemuan 12

✅ Multiple frameworks provide different perspectives → Use complementary
✅ SWOT/PESTEL untuk external analysis → Understand environment
✅ Porter's Forces untuk competitive analysis → Identify threats/opportunities
✅ CSF untuk strategic focus → Clarify "must win" battles

**1. Isu Etika dalam TI**

**A. Privacy & Perlindungan Data Pribadi**
- Organisasi menangani data sensitif: identitas, finansial, kesehatan, perilaku

- Tension: Software berlisensi mahal vs. tekanan cost
- Temptation: Gunakan cracked/unlicensed software
- Risiko: Jika ketahuan → reputasi rusak, denda besar
- Rekomendasi: Audit lisensi berkala, use open-source, budget untuk licenses

**C. Transparansi Algoritma & AI Bias**
- Black box AI: Pengguna tidak tahu mengapa keputusan made
- Bias algoritma: Model AI bisa discriminate protected groups
- Contoh: Credit scoring AI yang discriminate perempuan/minorities
- Rekomendasi: Explainability, fairness audit, human in the loop, appeal process

**D. Tanggung Jawab Sosial**
- Inklusi digital: Accessible untuk semua (elderly, disabled, low-literacy)
- Financial literacy: Edukasi pengguna tentang safe digital
- Anti-fraud: Prevent scams & misuse
- Environmental: Sustainability dalam IT operations

**2. Kebijakan TI**

**A. Information Security Policy**
- Scope: Data classification, access control, encryption, password policy
- Incident response: Definition, timeline, escalation, post-incident review
- Third-party risk: Vendor assessment, security audit, SLA requirements

**B. Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)**
- Email usage: Business only, no promotional/offensive content
- Internet usage: No adult content, gambling, file-sharing
- BYOD: Approval required, encryption mandatory, remote wipe capability
- System access: No password sharing, auto-logout, no tailgating

**C. Data Privacy & Protection Policy**
- Legal basis: Consent, contract, legal obligation, legitimate interest
- Data collection: Purpose limitation, minimization, accuracy
- Data subject rights: Access, correction, deletion, portability, objection
- Data breach: Notification timeline (72 hours), transparent communication

**D. Change Management Policy**
- Change categories: Emergency (fast-track), Standard (regular process), Minor (simplified)
- Process: Submit → Review → Approval → Testing → Implementation → Rollback prep
- Change window: Scheduled maintenance windows, user communication
- Documentation: Audit trail, effectiveness review

**3. Keamanan Informasi**

**A. CIA Triad**

| Element | Definition | Implementation |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Confidentiality** | Data not accessible to unauthorized | Encryption, access control, authentication |
| **Integrity** | Data not modified/corrupted | Data validation, digital signatures, version control |
| **Availability** | System accessible when needed | Redundancy, backup, disaster recovery, 99.99% target |

**B. Ancaman Utama**

1. **Phishing & Social Engineering**
   - Attack: Email yang kelihatan dari legitimate source, fake login page
   - Mitigation: Email security gateway, training, reporting mechanism

2. **Ransomware & Malware**
   - Attack: Encrypted files, system unavailable, business disruption
   - Mitigation: Endpoint security, sandbox analysis, network segmentation, offline backup

3. **Insider Threats**
   - Attack: Malicious/negligent employee steal/damage data
   - Mitigation: Access control, monitoring, training, background check, separation of duties

4. **DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)**
   - Attack: Massive traffic untuk unavailability
   - Mitigation: DDoS service, rate limiting, redundancy, incident response

5. **Data Breach & Unauthorized Access**
   - Attack: Exploit vulnerabilities, brute force, SQL injection
   - Mitigation: Vulnerability management, patching, WAF, database monitoring

**C. Defense in Depth Strategy**

```
Layer 1: PREVENTIVE CONTROLS
├─ Strong authentication (MFA)
├─ Encryption (at rest & in transit)
├─ Access controls & segregation of duties
└─ Security awareness training

Layer 2: DETECTIVE CONTROLS
├─ Security monitoring & logging
├─ Intrusion detection
├─ Behavior analytics
└─ Regular security audits

Layer 3: CORRECTIVE CONTROLS
├─ Incident response procedures
├─ Data recovery & backup
├─ Post-incident reviews
└─ Continuous improvement
```

# PERTEMUAN 14: PENYUSUNAN PORTOFOLIO APLIKASI TI (McFARLAN STRATEGIC GRID)

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Mengklasifikasikan aplikasi TI berdasarkan kontribusinya terhadap bisnis menggunakan McFarlan Strategic Grid untuk optimisasi investasi.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Konsep Portofolio Aplikasi TI**

**Definisi:** Kumpulan sistem/aplikasi yang mendukung strategi bisnis organisasi

**Tujuan:**
- Optimize investasi TI allocation
- Prioritize pengembangan & investasi
- Balance inovasi dengan operational stability
- Hindari pemborosan pada aplikasi non-strategic

**2. McFarlan Strategic Grid**

**Dua Dimensi:**

| Dimensi | Axis | Definition |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **Current Operational Impact** | Y-axis (Vertikal) | Seberapa critical aplikasi untuk operasi bisnis saat ini? |
| **Strategic Future Impact** | X-axis (Horizontal) | Seberapa penting aplikasi untuk strategy masa depan? |

**Empat Kuadran:**

```
HIGH STRATEGIC FUTURE IMPACT
        ↑
        │      HIGH POTENTIAL      │      STRATEGIC
        │    (Explore & Invest)    │  (Invest & Prioritize)
        │                          │
────────┼──────────────────────────┼─── HIGH OPERATIONAL IMPACT
        │                          │
        │     SUPPORT              │   KEY OPERATIONAL
        │  (Maintain & Optimize)   │  (Maintain & Upgrade)
        │                          │
        └──────────────────────────→
    LOW OPERATIONAL IMPACT        HIGH OPERATIONAL IMPACT
```

**3. Klasifikasi Empat Kuadran**

**QUADRANT 1: STRATEGIC**
- Karakteristik: High current impact + High strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: E-commerce platform, mobile banking app, core CRM
- Strategy: **Invest & Prioritize**
  - Continuous enhancement & optimization
  - Regular upgrades & patches
  - Ensure high availability (99.9%+ uptime)
  - Integrate dengan strategic initiatives

**QUADRANT 2: HIGH POTENTIAL**
- Karakteristik: Low current impact + High strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: AI analytics, blockchain, new digital services
- Strategy: **Explore, Pilot & Invest Strategically**
  - Accelerate development & deployment
  - Allocate best talent untuk innovation
  - Pilot projects untuk test feasibility
  - Fast-track high-value initiatives

**QUADRANT 3: KEY OPERATIONAL**
- Karakteristik: High current impact + Low strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: Legacy POS system, old HCM, traditional inventory system
- Strategy: **Maintain & Plan Upgrade**
  - Keep running dengan minimal investment
  - Plan replacement/modernization
  - Ensure continuity & reliability
  - Document technical debt
  - Prepare upgrade roadmap

**QUADRANT 4: SUPPORT**
- Karakteristik: Low current impact + Low strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: Simple admin tools, old file systems
- Strategy: **Maintain & Optimize**
  - Minimize investment
  - Consider consolidation/retirement
  - Low priority untuk new features
  - Cost optimization focus

**4. Strategi Pengelolaan Portofolio**

**Investment Strategy Per Kuadran:**

| Kuadran | Priority | Investment | Focus | Timeline |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------|----------|
| Strategic | HIGHEST | Necessary & justified | Reliability, optimization, integration | Continuous |
| High Potential | HIGH | Growth investment | Innovation, scale, differentiation | Accelerated |
| Key Operational | MEDIUM | Minimize cost | Reliability, compliance | Plan replacement |
| Support | LOWEST | Minimum | Cost reduction | As needed |

**Portfolio Balancing:**
- Strategic: 40-45% dari budget (foundation)
- Key Operational: 35-40% dari budget (maintenance)
- High Potential: 15-20% dari budget (growth)
- Support: 5-10% dari budget (minimal)

**5. Portfolio Roadmap - 3 Phases**

**Phase 1: Quick Wins & Foundation (M1-6)**
- Strategic: Enhance existing, improve performance
- High Potential: Launch pilots, accelerate innovation
- Budget: 20% dari total 2-year investment

**Phase 2: Strategic Acceleration (M7-18)**
- Strategic: Scale proven initiatives
- High Potential: Expand successful pilots
- Key Operational: Plan modernization
- Budget: 50% dari total 2-year investment

**Phase 3: Modernization (M19-30)**
- Key Operational: Execute replacements
- Strategic: Maintain & optimize
- High Potential: New wave innovations
- Budget: 30% dari total 2-year investment

### Key Insights Pertemuan 14

✅ McFarlan Grid provides systematic way untuk classify & manage portfolio
✅ Optimal investment allocation requires clear classification
✅ Balance antara innovation & operational stability critical
✅ Portfolio must evolve dengan changing business needs
✅ Regular reviews untuk update classification & strategy
