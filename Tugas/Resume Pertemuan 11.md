# PERTEMUAN 11: STUDI KASUS SEKTOR PUBLIK DAN PRIVAT

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Membandingkan dan menganalisis strategi TI di sektor publik vs sektor privat, dengan memahami drivers, challenges, dan best practices masing-masing.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Perbandingan Strategis (10 Dimensi)**

| Dimensi | Sektor Publik | Sektor Privat |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| **Fokus Utama** | Pelayanan publik, efisiensi | Inovasi, keunggulan kompetitif |
| **Stakeholder** | Masyarakat, pemerintah, regulators | Customers, investors, employees |
| **Dorongan Utama** | Regulasi & kebijakan | Market demand & kompetisi |
| **Fleksibilitas** | Rendah (terikat regulasi) | Tinggi (market-driven) |
| **Innovation Pace** | Lambat (cautious) | Cepat (fail-fast) |
| **Budget Model** | Fixed allocation | Growth-based, performance-linked |
| **Talent Approach** | Civil service rules | Market-competitive |
| **Success Metric** | Service quality, compliance | Revenue, market share |
| **Technology Choice** | Proven, stable | Cutting-edge, agile |
| **Risk Tolerance** | Low | Medium-High |

**2. Kecepatan Implementasi**

| Aspek | Publik | Privat | Impact |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| **Innovation pace** | 12-24 bulan | 2-4 minggu | Privat 10x lebih cepat |
| **Decision cycle** | 2-4 minggu | 1-3 hari | Privat 10x lebih cepat |
| **Budget approval** | 3-6 bulan | 1-2 minggu | Publik jauh lebih lambat |
| **Technology adoption** | 3-5 tahun | 6-12 bulan | Privat 5x lebih cepat |

**3. Resource Allocation**

**Sektor Publik:**
- Budget: Fixed annual allocation
- Utilization: Long-term planning
- Efficiency: Service improvement focus
- Risk tolerance: Very low

**Sektor Privat:**
- Budget: Performance-based, growth-driven
- Utilization: Flexible, rapid pivots
- Efficiency: Revenue/growth metrics
- Risk tolerance: High (venture capital model)

### Studi Kasus Publik: Jakarta Smart City

**Organisasi:** Pemerintah Provinsi DKI Jakarta (10 juta penduduk)

**Program Overview:**
- Perda DKI No. 1/2015
- Budget awal: Rp 2,8 Triliun (2015-2019)
- Focus areas: Transportasi, lingkungan, layanan publik, keamanan

**Strategic Initiatives:**

1. **Integrated Data Platform (Data Kota Jakarta)**
   - Centralized data warehouse untuk 23 OPD
   - Integration: traffic sensors, air quality, health, education, safety
   - Real-time monitoring dashboards
   - Expected: Better decision-making, coordinated policies

2. **IoT Infrastructure Deployment**
   - 10.000+ CCTV cameras dengan IoT capability
   - Environmental sensors (air quality, water, noise)
   - Disaster monitoring (flood, earthquake)
   - Smart traffic lights dengan adaptive signal control
   - Expected: Better visibility, faster response

3. **Digital Services Platform (Ayo Jakarta App)**
   - Citizen portal untuk akses layanan publik
   - Service request & tracking
   - Citizen feedback & complaints
   - Community engagement
   - Expected: Improved user experience, better feedback loop

**Implementation Roadmap:**

**Phase 1: Foundation (Y1-2)** - Target 50-60%
- Data governance, central warehouse
- Integration dengan 5 critical systems
- 3.000 IoT sensors deployed
- Ayo Jakarta beta launch
- Expected: Traffic -10%, service response -20%, satisfaction +15%

**Phase 2: Acceleration (Y3-4)** - Target 70-75%
- Integration 15+ additional systems
- Advanced analytics & AI
- Real-time decision support
- 7.000 additional IoT units
- Full Ayo Jakarta launch
- Expected: Traffic +25%, emergency response -30%, service access +40%

**Phase 3: Optimization (Y5+)** - Target 80%+
- Edge computing & real-time analytics
- Autonomous systems, predictive analytics
- Inter-city data sharing
- Emerging tech adoption
- Expected: Become model smart city, major quality of life improvements

### Studi Kasus Privat: Gojek

**Organisasi:** Gojek (Unicorn, 68M users, 2M+ daily transactions)

**Business Model:** Multi-service super-app
- Services: GoRide, GoFood, GoPay, GoShop, GoSend, GoMed, GoLife
- Technology-driven, data-centric operations
- Real-time matching algorithms
- Dynamic pricing & demand forecasting

**Strategic Initiatives:**

1. **Super-App Ecosystem**
   - Converge multiple services dalam 1 app
   - Network effects dari multi-service platform
   - Customer lifetime value maximization
   - Ecosystem benefits: convenience, data leverage, supply efficiency

2. **Technology & Data Strategy**
   - Cloud-native architecture (microservices)
   - Petabyte-scale data warehouse
   - Real-time analytics platform
   - ML/AI untuk personalization, pricing, matching
   - Expected: Better matching, personalization, pricing, fraud detection

3. **Innovation & Speed Advantage**
   - Agile development, quick market tests
   - Data-driven decision making
   - Competitive tech talent (market salaries)
   - Venture capital funding → aggressive innovation
   - Expected: Out-innovate competitors, capture market opportunities

**Product Launch Playbook (10 weeks):**
- W1-2: Market research, concept validation
- W3-4: MVP development
- W5: Beta launch (10K users)
- W6: Feedback iteration
- W7: Full launch (100%)
- W8-10: Scale & optimize
- **vs Traditional: 6-12 months (6-12x lebih lama)**

**Data Monetization Strategies:**
1. Transaction commissions (20-25%)
2. Advertising (10-15% growing)
3. Fintech services (lending, insurance, investment)
4. B2B data insights & consulting (emerging)

### Key Insights Pertemuan 11

✅ Context drives strategy → Publik & privat fundamentally different drivers
✅ Speed vs stability trade-off → Publik perlu careful governance; privat perlu speed
✅ Convergence possible → Hybrid approaches combining best of both
✅ Learn from each other → Publik adopt agility; privat adopt sustainability
✅ Regulatory environment matters → Different constraints & opportunities
