
# PERTEMUAN 14: PENYUSUNAN PORTOFOLIO APLIKASI TI (McFARLAN STRATEGIC GRID)

## Ringkasan Materi

**Topik Utama:** Mengklasifikasikan aplikasi TI berdasarkan kontribusinya terhadap bisnis menggunakan McFarlan Strategic Grid untuk optimisasi investasi.

### Konsep Kunci

**1. Konsep Portofolio Aplikasi TI**

**Definisi:** Kumpulan sistem/aplikasi yang mendukung strategi bisnis organisasi

**Tujuan:**
- Optimize investasi TI allocation
- Prioritize pengembangan & investasi
- Balance inovasi dengan operational stability
- Hindari pemborosan pada aplikasi non-strategic

**2. McFarlan Strategic Grid**

**Dua Dimensi:**

| Dimensi | Axis | Definition |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **Current Operational Impact** | Y-axis (Vertikal) | Seberapa critical aplikasi untuk operasi bisnis saat ini? |
| **Strategic Future Impact** | X-axis (Horizontal) | Seberapa penting aplikasi untuk strategy masa depan? |

**Empat Kuadran:**

```
HIGH STRATEGIC FUTURE IMPACT
        ↑
        │      HIGH POTENTIAL      │      STRATEGIC
        │    (Explore & Invest)    │  (Invest & Prioritize)
        │                          │
────────┼──────────────────────────┼─── HIGH OPERATIONAL IMPACT
        │                          │
        │     SUPPORT              │   KEY OPERATIONAL
        │  (Maintain & Optimize)   │  (Maintain & Upgrade)
        │                          │
        └──────────────────────────→
    LOW OPERATIONAL IMPACT        HIGH OPERATIONAL IMPACT
```

**3. Klasifikasi Empat Kuadran**

**QUADRANT 1: STRATEGIC**
- Karakteristik: High current impact + High strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: E-commerce platform, mobile banking app, core CRM
- Strategy: **Invest & Prioritize**
  - Continuous enhancement & optimization
  - Regular upgrades & patches
  - Ensure high availability (99.9%+ uptime)
  - Integrate dengan strategic initiatives

**QUADRANT 2: HIGH POTENTIAL**
- Karakteristik: Low current impact + High strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: AI analytics, blockchain, new digital services
- Strategy: **Explore, Pilot & Invest Strategically**
  - Accelerate development & deployment
  - Allocate best talent untuk innovation
  - Pilot projects untuk test feasibility
  - Fast-track high-value initiatives

**QUADRANT 3: KEY OPERATIONAL**
- Karakteristik: High current impact + Low strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: Legacy POS system, old HCM, traditional inventory system
- Strategy: **Maintain & Plan Upgrade**
  - Keep running dengan minimal investment
  - Plan replacement/modernization
  - Ensure continuity & reliability
  - Document technical debt
  - Prepare upgrade roadmap

**QUADRANT 4: SUPPORT**
- Karakteristik: Low current impact + Low strategic impact
- Aplikasi: Contoh: Simple admin tools, old file systems
- Strategy: **Maintain & Optimize**
  - Minimize investment
  - Consider consolidation/retirement
  - Low priority untuk new features
  - Cost optimization focus

**4. Strategi Pengelolaan Portofolio**

**Investment Strategy Per Kuadran:**

| Kuadran | Priority | Investment | Focus | Timeline |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------|----------|
| Strategic | HIGHEST | Necessary & justified | Reliability, optimization, integration | Continuous |
| High Potential | HIGH | Growth investment | Innovation, scale, differentiation | Accelerated |
| Key Operational | MEDIUM | Minimize cost | Reliability, compliance | Plan replacement |
| Support | LOWEST | Minimum | Cost reduction | As needed |

**Portfolio Balancing:**
- Strategic: 40-45% dari budget (foundation)
- Key Operational: 35-40% dari budget (maintenance)
- High Potential: 15-20% dari budget (growth)
- Support: 5-10% dari budget (minimal)

**5. Portfolio Roadmap - 3 Phases**

**Phase 1: Quick Wins & Foundation (M1-6)**
- Strategic: Enhance existing, improve performance
- High Potential: Launch pilots, accelerate innovation
- Budget: 20% dari total 2-year investment

**Phase 2: Strategic Acceleration (M7-18)**
- Strategic: Scale proven initiatives
- High Potential: Expand successful pilots
- Key Operational: Plan modernization
- Budget: 50% dari total 2-year investment

**Phase 3: Modernization (M19-30)**
- Key Operational: Execute replacements
- Strategic: Maintain & optimize
- High Potential: New wave innovations
- Budget: 30% dari total 2-year investment

### Key Insights Pertemuan 14

✅ McFarlan Grid provides systematic way untuk classify & manage portfolio
✅ Optimal investment allocation requires clear classification
✅ Balance antara innovation & operational stability critical
✅ Portfolio must evolve dengan changing business needs
✅ Regular reviews untuk update classification & strategy
